Fire accidents can occur unexpectedly and escalate within seconds. Understanding fire safety not only protects lives but also minimizes property loss and environmental damage.

Awareness, preparation, and quick action are the keys to preventing small hazards from turning into major disasters. Knowing how to respond during emergencies can save your life and the lives of others.
